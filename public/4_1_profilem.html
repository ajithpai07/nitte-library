Help Section



Hello,






After landing on the website, click on the sign-up option in the rightmost end. There, you can sign-up if you are a new user and want to be a member of the library. Click here to get redirected to the Sign-Up page.

Please submit an application to the librarian for the deletion of a membership, any outstanding amount - the balance amount in the account will need to be paid/refunded, and the librarian shall delete the account.

Firstly, log in to the account and click on 'Profile Management' over there and choose the update profile option to update your details.

After logging in to your account, click on the 'Profile Management' which is present at the top-right and there you can view your data.

The overdue books can be seen in the books owed section of 'Booking'. Also, there is an option of book renewal.

A user can take as many books as needed to check out. And, all the books available can be checked out.

1. You need to add the basic details like Name, Date of Birth, Email, Phone Number, Password and choose a file to upload your selfie.
2. And again, Address details like Door/Flat number, Street name, City and Postal Code.
3. It also asks for your book preferences, where at least one preference is mandatory.
4. Finally, It asks for your KYC details such as KYC id number and type which are required for the KYC approval.

In the 'Booking' menu, choose the previous booking option to get the list of all books checked out.

"Takshashila Library" is used by anyone with interest in the wider world. Students, teachers, scholars etc. may approach it in different ways, but there is something of interest for everyone.
