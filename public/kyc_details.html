What are the requirements of the documents to be submitted for KYC?



Selfie
- Photo of yourself, with your entire face visible.
- Remove any accessories such as glasses when taking your picture.
- The photo should be well lit and in focus.
- The photo of an existing picture or computer screen is not suitable.

Proof of Identity
- Identification documents can be one of the following: ID card, passport or driving license.
- The document should include full name, date of birth and a picture of yourself.
- The photo should cover the full document and clearly show the required information.
- The photo should be well lit and in focus.

Proof of Address
- Proof of address document can be one of the following: bank/credit card statement or a utility bill.
- Electricity, Gas, Landline and Water utility bills are acceptable. GSM, TV/Satellite, Internet utility bills are NOT suitable.
- The document should include full name, address, document date and name of the issuer.
- The document should be issued within three months of the current date.
- The photo should cover the full document and clearly show the required information.
- The photo should be well lit and in focus.
